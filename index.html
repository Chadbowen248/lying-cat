Lying Cat

Sidekick

A tiger-sized, hairless blue Sphynx cat, and partner of the intergalactic bounty hunter called The Will.

Loyal

Lying Cat likes to be loved, makes funny faces, reacts on her masters voice and feelings, and doesn't like to be ignored.

"Lying!"

She has the ability to detect when a verbal statement is a lie, which she indicates by saying, "Lying".
